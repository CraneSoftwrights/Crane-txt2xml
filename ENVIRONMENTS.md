# Known Crane-txt2xml environments

Crane Softwrights provides reference implementations for specific XML vocabularies:

- **[Crane-txt2ubl](Crane-txt2ubl/README.md)** — Universal Business Language (UBL) documents such as invoices and orders
- **[PubNoteIn-txt2xml](https://github.com/RealtaOnline/PubNote?tab=readme-ov-file#readme)** — PubMed article submission metadata, with natural-language label support in multiple languages

These serve both as usable environments and as examples for implementers creating their own.
